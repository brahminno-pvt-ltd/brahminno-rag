About Brahminno
---------------
Brahminno Pvt Ltd is an AI and immersive technology(Xr & Gaming) innovation company helping startups, enterprises, and governments build next-generation digital products.

We specialize in Generative AI, AI Agents, Machine Learning, XR (AR/VR/MR), Gaming, IoT, and Digital Twin systems, enabling organizations to transform ideas into scalable intelligent platforms.

Our team combines deep engineering expertise with creative innovation to design and develop advanced solutions such as:

• Generative AI applications and LLM-powered platforms
• AI agents and automation systems
• AI video analytics and smart city solutions
• XR (AR/VR) training simulations and immersive experiences
• Game development and interactive environments
• IoT-enabled smart systems and predictive analytics
• Rapid AI prototype and MVP development for startups

At Brahminno, we believe the future belongs to intelligent, immersive, and connected systems. Our mission is to help organizations accelerate innovation by building powerful AI-driven solutions that deliver real-world impact.
